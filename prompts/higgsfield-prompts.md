# Max-O-Meter — Higgsfield Soul Prompts

Use your existing Soul ID character. **Settings to keep consistent across all 8:**

- **Aspect ratio:** 1:1 (square — works as a collectable card)
- **Style:** Photorealistic
- **Framing:** Tight chest-up portrait, head centred, looking directly at camera
- **Lighting:** Studio softbox, slight rim light, clean shadow
- **Background:** Solid colour gradient (specified per card to match the mood)
- **Quality:** Sharp, high detail, magazine cover quality

**File naming:** Save each as the listed filename and drop into `~/Projects/personal/max-o-meter/images/`
